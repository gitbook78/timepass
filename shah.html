Shah Rukh Khan

The King of Bollywood

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, is a legendary actor in Bollywood. He has appeared in many iconic movies and is loved worldwide for his charisma and talent.

Known as the "King of Bollywood," SRK has inspired millions with his journey from Delhi to stardom. Some of his famous movies include Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge and My Name is Khan.

Beyond acting, SRK owns a cricket team and is known for his humility and generosity.
