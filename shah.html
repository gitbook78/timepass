Shah Rukh Khan

The King of Bollywood

Shah Rukh Khan, popularly known as SRK, is an Indian actor and producer. He is called the "King of Bollywood" for his legendary roles and unmatched charisma. Over the years, he has starred in over 80 films and has become a global icon.

Some of his most famous movies include *Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge*, *Kuch Kuch Hota Hai*, and *Chennai Express*. His journey from a regular boy in Delhi to a superstar has inspired millions of people worldwide.

Beyond acting, Shah Rukh Khan owns a cricket team and runs a production company. His dedication, humility, and love for his fans make him a true legend.
